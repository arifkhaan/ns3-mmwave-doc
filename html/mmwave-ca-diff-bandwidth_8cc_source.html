21 /* In this example, a single UE is connected with a single MmWave BS. The
22  * UE is randomly placed inside a circle with radius of 150m and it moves with
23  * random velocity between 2m/s and 4m/s. The BS is placed in the center with
24  * height 15m. The system bandwidth is fixed at 1GHz. If CA is enabled, 2 CCs
25  * are used and the total bandwidth is divided among the two according to
26  * the bandDiv parameter, which is equal to the ratio between the badwidth
27  * allocated to CC0 and the bandwidth allocated to CC1.
28  */
